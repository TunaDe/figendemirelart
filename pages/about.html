I graduated from Hacettepe University Faculty of Dentistry in 1986. I completed all my academic studies and academic promotions at the same university and became a professor in 2004. I started my painting works in 2012. I took drawing, oil painting and watercolor lessons for four years, and drawing and engraving lessons for the following two years. I held my first personal exhibition in 2016. Later, I participated in art fairs and group exhibitions. I illustrated some scientific books and magazines in the field of dentistry. I continue my work in my own workshop.
